Troops

Troops are offensive units that are used to attack the villages of other players.
However, they can also be found on defense in certain Buildings, such as the Clan Castle and Guard Post.
There are a variety of different troops that can be trained, and each has its own strengths and weaknesses.

Elixir Troops

Elixir troops are trained in the regular Barracks. Upgrading the Barracks can unlock more Elixir troops.
You can also research Elixir troops in the Laboratory to make them more powerful.

  • Barbarian

    This fearless warrior relies on his bulging muscles and striking mustache to wreak havoc in enemy
    villages. Release a horde of Barbarians and enjoy the mayhem!
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 1

  • Archer

    These sharpshooters like to keep their distance on the battlefield and in life. Nothing makes them
    happier than single-mindedly taking down their target.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 2

  • Giant

    These big guys may seem calm, but show them a turret or cannon and you'll see their fury unleashed! Slow
    yet durable, these warriors are best used to soak up hits.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 3

  • Goblin

    These pesky little creatures only have eyes for one thing: LOOT! They are faster than a Spring Trap, and
    their hunger for resources is limitless.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 4

  • Wall Breaker

    Nothing warms a Wall Breaker's cold and undead heart like blowing up walls. A squad of them will make
    way for your ground units, and they will do it with a BANG!
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 5

  • Balloon

    These promoted skeletons have traded in their joy of destroying walls for a joy of destroying defenses.
    Deploy them to take out pesky mortars and cannons!
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 6

  • Wizard

    The Wizard is a terrifying presence on the battlefield. Pair him up with some of his fellows and cast
    concentrated blasts of destruction on anything, land or sky!
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 7

  • Healer

    This majestic creature lives to protect and aid her fellow troops. Any army is improved with her healing
    support, but make sure to protect her from air defenses!
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 8

  • Dragon

    The might of the dragons are known throughout the land. This scaly terror of the skies feels no mercy
    and nothing will escape the fiery splashes of his breath.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 9

  • Pekka

    Is P.E.K.K.A a knight? A samurai? A robot? No one knows! P.E.K.K.A's armor absorbs even the mightiest of blows.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 10

  • Baby Dragon

    This fire-breathing hatchling is shy around other air units, but leave it alone and it will throw a fit!
    When not around other air units, Baby Dragons become enraged and gain bonus damage and attack speed.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 11

  • Miner

    These sneaky shovelers burrow underground, pass beneath walls, and pop up right next to their
    targets. While underground, Miners cannot be damaged and will not trigger traps, but still gain bonuses
    from Spells.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 12

  • Electro Dragon

    Possessing iron-tough scales and a breath of devastating lightning, the Electro Dragon's favorite
    thing is raining destruction from above. When vanquished, the Electro Dragon even pummels the
    ground with lightning strikes!
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 13

  • Yeti

    The heavy-hitting furry fellow digs cold weather and his Yetimite buddies. Hurt him, and you'll make
    the Yetimites real angry.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 14

  • Dragon Rider

    This aerial engine of destruction readily and frankly, quite excitedly lays waste to any defense it comes
    across. The skeleton onboard swears he's not touching the controls.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 15

  • Electro Titan

    Barely controlling her raw magical power, the Electro Titan is a formidable force on the battlefield. She
    channels some of her magic into a powerful electro whip while the rest spills out to damage everything
    around her.
    Unlocks on Barracks Level 16
